## Overview

Giggin is an innovative web application designed to revolutionize live concert
experiences by creating captivating smartphone light shows and facilitating
interactive audience engagement features. This groundbreaking platform aims to
enhance the enjoyment of concert-goers, elevating their experiences to
unprecedented levels.

The Giggin app offers a seamless and user-friendly interface for both audience
members and event organizers, providing a dynamic and immersive environment
during live performances.

  ## Solution

  <div class="flex flex-wrap gap-10 items-center mb-10">

    <div class='min-w-[200px] flex-1 order-1'>
      ### Audience App 
    
      Built using React, the Audience App offers concert attendees **easy access** via a QR code scan.

      Once inside, users can immerse themselves in mesmerizing **light shows** synchronized perfectly with the music.

      Additionally, the app features a dedicated landing page offering exclusive content related to the concert, including artist **promotions**, merchandise, and music.

      ### Live App Control

      Another React-based web application, the Live App Control, grants artists and
      gig promoters **real-time control** over the audience experience.

      Through this interface, organizers can curate content displayed on the Audience
      App, ensuring a dynamic and engaging performance.

      Furthermore, the Live App Control includes a **metronome feature** to assist
      musicians during live performances if needed.
